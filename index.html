Why Our Foundation

By donating at DonasyonPH, you are not just helping one person or one city, but countless people who needed you're help and care.
2020 has been a challenge for us in different aspects, on the other hand, there are countless people who do not have anything that makes us want to help them for a better future. We do not just work, but helps to encourage, protect, and support them in their lives.
If our team, DonasyonPH, and you will work together, everyone can strive for happiness and fair life, as well as a better future for our country.

 We are Trustworthy
DonasyonPH is a trustworthy team that helps people in need. We do not have the authority to use your donations for our own desires and not even a single cent will be placed in our pockets. As well as that, DonasyonPH will use your donations directly to our donation drives and aims to provide relief/essential goods to lend a hand to those who needed help. Donating can give people a sense of hope through this act of kindness and generosity. Every peso donated from the heart is worthy of every utmost thanks.
We serve
We serve people because we all deserve to have a fair and better life, as well as to have a better future for our country. We serve to fight underprivileged life for the people who do not have the basic supplies that are needed in order to survive. We also serve to gave them what they needed and to have their life with happiness that they are also important.
  Our Goals
Our goal is to help, but not just to help but to protect, support, and encourage people who needed it for their better future. As well as to give their basic supplies needed to survive, like shelter, food, and water. Along with, preparing an emergency involves risk monitoring, planning, and establishing how to deliver assistance and protection.
